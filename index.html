Welcome to the HRI Public Deployment Checklist!

We created this tool to help researchers identify potential blind spots in their deployment and make the deployment smoother. We hope it covers all aspects of the deployment, from the robot's technical details to research procedures, and to public and site interaction.

Authors anonymous due to double blind submission process. Questions and feedback can be addressed anonymously to hriiprobot@gmail.com.

Contributing Checklist Items and Anecdotes

Share concrete checklist items, short deployment anecdotes, broader suggestions, or notes about missing topics.

Choose a path

  • Your own experience/study: create a branch with proposed checklist additions or edits.
  • Make broader suggestions: open an issue for comments, concerns, missing areas, or discussion.

Checklist item guidance

Items should be practical, specific, and actionable. Prefer verbs like test, confirm, identify, document, prepare, locate, verify, coordinate, or review.

Anecdote format

  • Deployment context: describe the site, robot, or interaction without identifying details.
  • What happened: describe the issue, surprise, workaround, or useful practice.
  • Why it matters: explain what future teams should learn.
  • Suggested checklist item: write one concise item to add or revise.

Privacy

For double-blind review, avoid author names, institutions, exact locations, participant details, identifying media, and private site documents.
